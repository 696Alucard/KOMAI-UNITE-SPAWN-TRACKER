HOW TO USE
🗺️ Map Selection
At the top of the screen, select the map you are currently playing — Groudon, Kyogre or Rayquaza. This changes the spawn events, the boss, and the visual theme.
Note: Regidrago does not appear on Rayquaza's map.
⏱️ Timer
The large timer counts down from 10:00. Tap it to edit it manually.
START — Start or resume the countdown.
PAUSE — Pause at any moment.
+1:00 +10s +1s — Jump forward in time.
-1s -10s -1:00 — Go back in time.
RESET — Reset to 10:00.
Use the adjustments to sync the tracker with the in-game clock if you started late.
🏰 Lane Status (Dynamic only)
Update the goalzone situation for TOP and BOT lanes independently. This affects the Swablu spawn cycle on each lane.

▲ ADV — Advantage: your team has destroyed an enemy goalzone on this lane. The enemy has one fewer goalzone than you.

■ EVEN — Even: both teams have destroyed one goalzone each on this lane. The balance is restored but both sides have lost a structure.

▼ DIS — Disadvantage: the enemy has destroyed one of your goalzones. Your team has one fewer goalzone on this lane.

No button selected = no goalzones destroyed yet → Swablu follow the Classic timing.
Tap the active button again to deselect it and return to Classic.
✅ CLEARED Button (Dynamic only)
When an objective appears in the SPAWNED panel, a ✓ CLEARED button appears for each clearable objective. Press it as soon as your team kills the objective.

Regieleki and Regi Ice/Rock/Steel — Clearing starts an exact 2-minute respawn timer. This is critical: if you don't press CLEARED, the tracker cannot calculate the next spawn time and you will miss the next window.

Swablu TOP Swablu BOT — Clearing updates the Swablu cycle based on the current lane state at that moment. The Swablu "see" the goalzone situation when they respawn, so timing your clear matters.
🔔 Alerts
45 seconds before each spawn, the event appears in NEXT 45s with the Pokémon's cry as an audio alert.
At spawn time, the cry plays again and the event moves to SPAWNED with a green blinking animation.
Events flashing red in NEXT 45s are spawning within 5 seconds.
